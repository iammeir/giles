12 postponing

Kia ora Koutou,

I suppose you’ve all heard the news now. As of midnight Wednesday, Aotearoa is going to alert level 4 in response to the COVID-19 pandemic.

A lot of things have changed or are going to change for the next month or so.

One of them is that I’m putting the newsletter on hold.

Another is that the estimated finish time of the caravan has been pushed back a bit.

A third is that I dropped my phone and damaged the camera’s focus mechanism, so I’m having trouble taking photos of our progress.

I know a lot of the people subscribed to this newsletter work in a particular essential industry - your work has always been vital, and never more than now. keep up the mahi uaua.

The rest of you, hole up, deep breaths, and continue routine buying habits.

The end of a matter is better than its beginning, and patience is better than pride. - Ecclesiastes 7:8

Ngā mihi, Giles
