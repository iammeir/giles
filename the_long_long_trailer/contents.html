The Long Long Trailer.

From the Start of 2020 until about October I wrote a newsletter that documented our renovation of an old, unbelievably long trailer.
In July/August 2021, in response to Google changing how it handles image sharing, I moved the hosting of the newsletter and images to my website. I also (significantly) compressed the images. This was a chance to learn more about scripting workflows, ways to make my digital footprint ecologically lighter, and exercise some long overdue semblance of stewardship of my online data.
