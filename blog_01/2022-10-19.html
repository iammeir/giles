I couldn’t understand why I was so irritable the past few days, after having two nights of heavily disrupted sleep. My nose is so close to my face I ignore it.

It turns out I’ve never had a nice asparagus. The archetype of the ideal snowpea, bursting in your mouth. It was so crisp, so tender. I was just born yesterday when I ate that asparagus.
