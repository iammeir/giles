Reading

The librarian complimented me on my wallet, I sort of wish I had more to say than ‘aw kia ora, yeah I like it.’ Maybe I should prepare an artist statement I can use next time.

Food

On the way home I stopped and bought gelato. It was every bit as good as I wanted it to be. My memory deceives but I think this didn’t use to happen as often for me: I’d anticipate an activity or event, carry it out and be disappointed, a little, as it didn’t meet my expectations. Now my expectations have changed, or my experiences have gotten better. Both are fine.
